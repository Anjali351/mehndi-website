About Me

Hi, I’m Anjali, a passionate Mehndi artist based in Mumbai. What started as a childhood hobby has now turned into a creative journey where I get to bring joy, beauty, and elegance to special occasions.

I specialize in Bridal Mehndi, Party Mehndi, Arabic Mehndi, and Festival Mehndi. Each design is crafted with love and precision to reflect your personality and the vibe of the occasion.

Over the years, I have created hundreds of unique mehndi patterns for weddings, engagements, and festive celebrations. My goal is simple: to make your moments unforgettable with designs that speak your story.

“Your hands tell a story — let’s make it beautiful together.”
